Who We Are?

We, Heroes and Legends, are a Christian community based in UC Berkeley who love to learn and spread the Word of God. We are also part of a larger community called International Full Gospel Fellowship who meet every Sunday in Pinole, CA. The guys call themselves Heroes, while the girls, who call themselves Legends. Primarily consisting of students and recent alumni, we enjoy sightseeing, movies, and boba! We meet once a week for a prayer and worship night in various locations around campus. We break into groups for weekly Bible Study and spend most of weekends hitting cafes, gyms, and libraries together. We believe that we are here to enjoy and share the love of God with one another, so what better way to do that than living our lives and making memories together.
