Who

Welcome to our community! We are a Christian community based in UC Berkeley who love to learn and spread the Word of God. Primarily consisting of students and recent alumni, we also enjoy hiking, sightseeing, and exploring local cafes and shops. We meet during weekdays for a quick prayer meeting and spend our weekends eating (always)... hitting the gym (occasionally)... and cramming for midterms (most of the time)... together! We have separate fellowships every other week: the guys, who call themselves Heroes, will have a bro-talk at one place while the girls, who call themselves Legends, are having their girls-night-out somewhere else. The point is, you will never feel bored or lonely here. Check out our upcoming events below and join us!

Care Group

"Care Group" is our way of calling the gathering that we have every Friday. It is a chance for us to get to know each other, try delicious meals made by our own master chefs, and more importantly, deepen our understanding about the Bible. Stay connected.
Sunday Service

We believe that prayers bring breakthrough, revival, and comfort in times of need. Every Thursday, we will spend sometimes to share our stories and struggles with one another and support each other in prayer. Come and feel the presence of the Lord.
Prayer Meeting

Our community is an extension of International Full Gospel Fellowship in Pinole. Every Sunday, we come together in a larger group to worship and hear the Word of God. Contact us for more information on the service time, venue and transport inquiries.
